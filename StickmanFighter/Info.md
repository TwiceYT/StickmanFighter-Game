## How to play the game:
	1. Select the amount of players in the game (2-4).
	2. Each player have the ability to jump, hit and block.
	3. Each player has three lives.
	4. Hit attack your opponents to knock them out of the map, and block to defend yourself from being hit by your opponents.
	5. You can only hit the opponent when you are on the same level as them, and you can only block when you are on the same level as them.
	6. The last player standing wins the game.

## Press F1 to open the debug screen:
	The debug screen shows you how many players uses the controllers / pads.
	If no controller / pad is being used, it show "NOT FOUND"
		This screen is useful to make sure all your controllers / pads are connected and found.

### Attack System

Holding the attack button charges the hit. A charge bar appears above the fighter's
head, going from yellow to red. Releasing at full charge deals maximum damage and
knockback. A fully charged hit at high damage percentage will send opponents flying
off the stage.
